    “‘To the devil I pitch all the nobiliy,’cries a little man, in a peculiar accent,‘I
am sure they have of late used me most scurvily. You must know, gentlemen, some
time ago, upon the arrival of a certain noble duke from his travels, I sat myself down,
and vamped up a fine flaunting poetical panegyric, which I had written in such a
strain, that I facied it would have even wheedled milk from a mouse. In this I
represented the whole kingdom welcoming his grace to his native soil, not forgetting
the loss France and Italy would sustain in their arts by his departure, I expected
to touch for a bank-bill at least; so folding up my verses in gilt paper, I gave my
last half-crown to his genteel servant to be the bearer. My letter was safely conveyed
to his grace, and the servant, after four hours absence, during which time I led the
life of a fiend, returned with a letter four times as big as mine. Guess my ecstasy
at the prospect of so fine a return. 1 eagerly took the packet into my hands, that
trembled to receive it. I kept it some time unopened before me, brooding over the
expected treasure it contained; when opening it, as I hope to be saved, gentlemen,
his grace had sent me in payment tor my poem, no bank-bills, but six copies of verses
each longer than mine, addressed to him upon the same occasion.’”
